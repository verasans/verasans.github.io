TRASHR

TRASHR will be a platform to allow you to search for the gargabe disposal bins nearby. You will also be notified when the garbage truck is coming by, so you never miss a pick up again.
You will also be able to add new locations to your profile and help TRASHR with your contributions. Every user will play a very important role, by sugesting new garbage disposal locations while being rewarded with unlocked achivements, for a more fun experience.
Since the app will only be available for Android users in the begining, I've followed the Material Design Guidelines created by Google. I wanted TRASHR to have a nice user experience with a friendly, intuitive and simple interface.
