du Sales Portal
Visual improvements to the existing portal

du Sales Portal is a portal for direct and indirect channels, that is dedicated to activate mobile numbers, manage offers and customer documentation. A platform that simplifies the customer sales processes. By streamlining recurring processes and focusing on relevant information, this portal was design to save time to its users.
For this project I've made some visual improvements and I've created the user manuals for new agents. Where they can find a brief text about DSP, how it works and step by step explanation of the processes for sells agents. In adition to the manual, I've made an interactive HTML+JS presentation of the project with explanatory videos.
