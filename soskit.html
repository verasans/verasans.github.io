S.O.S dyslexia kit - for parents and children

Project by Sara Miguel for UTAD - University of Trás-os-Montes e Alto Douro, Vila Real, Portugal.
Texts by Sara Miguel
Illustration and edition by me
- This handbook is part of a kit, as well as some games and challenges, that was created to help children with dyslexia and their parents to deal with this difficulty.
The book tells a story about a girl named Lexia, and her only friend, Mr. Green, a ball of green wool. Lexia has no friends at school, other kids think she's different, so, Mr. Green is helping Lexia surpass her difficulty.
